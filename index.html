What is Viaje Musica?
Hi, my name is
Temitope Aina.
Temitope Aina.
Music has been part of my life for as long as I can remember. Every key I touch, every sound I create, comes from memories and emotions that have shaped who I am. A place of real stories, real moments, real me.

Viaje Música is my way of sharing that with you. It’s not perfect. It’s personal. And it’s powerful..
This is
VIAJE MUSICA
So… What Is Viaje Música?
It’s a concert, yes— but not the kind you just watch. This is something you feel.

Viaje musica brings together classical music and African Music in a shared space. VIAJE MUSICA invites you on an enchanting musical journey where music breathes in colors. from stirring solos to thrilling and dynamic trios , to the powerful rhythms of African drums and beyond.

Viaje is more than a stage.
