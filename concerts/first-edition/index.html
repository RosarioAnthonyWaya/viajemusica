The Beginning of Something Beautiful.

Viaje Musica
The very first edition of Viaje Música
was nothing short of special
was nothing short of special
Held in 2021, it introduced a new kind of concert — one that respected structure, yet made space for freedom; one that honoured classical form, yet gave room for African expression.

From Temitope Aina’s solo piano pieces to ensemble arrangements that featured young, gifted vocalists and instrumentalists, the night set the tone for what Viaje would become — a home for serious musicians, honest sound, and intentional creativity.
